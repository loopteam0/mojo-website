About Mojo Born out of a personal experience in the heart of Lebanon, Mojo emerged as more than just a mobile wallet—it's a beacon of hope, bringing financial freedom and innovation to the Lebanese people. In 2021, a couple of our co-founders found themselves in a predicament, unable to pay for a dinner during their Lebanese holiday due to the limitations of the prevailing financial situation. This singular event, not only highlighted an everyday problem but also ignited a passion to develop a solution. With over a decade of fintech expertise across Africa, we channelled our insights and commitment into creating Mojo; a platform designed to serve the greater good of Lebanon, redfining the future of finance in Lebanon. Our mission is to provide a secure, efficient, flexible, and affordable wallet for every citizen, making daily transactions—from buying groceries to settling utility bills—a seamless experience. Moreover, Mojo aims to reintegrate the Lebanese community into the global financial ecosystem, rectifying the exclusion we faced post 17 October Revoltuion. Together, we mark this Anniversary by introducting Mojo on this significate date. Let us regain our financial freedom, let’s empower Lebanon once again, let’s get our Mojo on!
